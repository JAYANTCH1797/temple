## Pillar 1: Deeptech Healthtech PM (Inito)

**Why this matters for Temple:** Inito is the closest professional proxy to what Temple is building. Both are:
- Hardware + software products that read biological signals
- Regulated health/wellness devices sold to end consumers
- Products where the data science layer IS the product

### Key stories to showcase:

#### A. Teleconsultation — Deep Research Product
- 70% of non-converters needed deeper product understanding
- Built an expert-led teleconsultation program
- 48% opt-in rate, 24% conversion lift
- **Temple parallel:** When the product is complex and deeply personal (body metrics), you need to think beyond UI — you need trust-building systems

#### B. LTV / Churn — Deep User Behavior
- Strip-driven LTV model — user behavior is cyclical (fertility cycles)
- Reduced churn by 8%, increased LTV by 3% through subscription redesign
- **Temple parallel:** Wearable retention is about habit loops and cyclical engagement — same mental model

#### C. Healthcare Complexity
- US market, FDA-adjacent considerations, sensitive user data
- Understanding the weight of getting health data wrong
- **Temple parallel:** Temple will face similar regulatory and trust challenges with physiological metrics

#### D. Sensor Data → Actionable Insight
- Inito reads hormone levels from urine strips → app interprets and guides
- The entire product is "raw biological signal → meaningful user action"
- **Temple parallel:** This is literally what Temple does — sensor data from body → actionable performance insight
