Inito · Teleconsultation
Women don't need information. They need to feel understood.
Problem: 70% of non-converters weren't buying despite seeing value.

Naive approach: Hire fertility experts, build a chat widget, connect users.

What actually happened: 25+ interviews with fertility coaches and OBGYNs. Mapped their communication patterns — the way they pause, validate, reframe anxiety as action.

Replicated those patterns into SOPs for consultation flow. Result: +24% conversion lift · 48% opt-in rate
Inito · LTV / Churn
Strips aren't inventory — they're the gating mechanism for value.
Problem: High churn after cycle 1-2. Users weren't coming back.

Naive approach: Push more strips, discount, remind users.

Real finding: The churn inflection isn't purchase or usage — it's ovulation confirmation. Women were running out of strips before their biological cycle reached ovulation.

Solution: Bundles sized to biological cycle length + psychology-based subscriptions (cancel anytime, refund unused). Result: Churn 18.5% → 17% · Subscription 4% → 10%
The Pattern
Go deeper than surface — the answers live in understanding, not obvious places.
Every initiative followed the same arc: surface-level thinking fails. The real answer requires going uncomfortably deep — into biology, psychology, behavior.

Athletes are identical. The insight that changes performance is almost never obvious. It lives in neuroscience, biomechanics, recovery biology — places most PMs won't go.

That's what I do. I go where the answers actually live.

The Depth Habit
"Fertility is where surface-level thinking fails. The only way to solve was going uncomfortably deep — studying hormone biology before touching Figma.""
Every problem at Inito looked simple on the surface. None of them were. The ones I solved best were the ones where I went deepest:
Fertility Assessment
Mapped user-reported signals → real hormonal pattern clusters. Researched which inputs meaningfully correlate with hormone behavior — cycle length variability, PMS severity, acne, luteal duration, TTC history, and diagnosed conditions.
Analyzed existing user hormone charts to identify recurring biological archetypes (clear ovulatory spike, delayed surge, multi-spike LH, weak luteal rise).
Built a rule-based engine that translated user signals into physiologically plausible sample charts — not guesses, but patterns grounded in real distributions. Each quiz path was research optimised, not just conversion-optimised.
Teleconsultation
Researched in depth on how expert handle complex fertility cases — how they build trust , how they solve for education with empathy.
